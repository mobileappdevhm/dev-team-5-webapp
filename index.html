Courses in English

A progressive web app for international students of Munich UAS

About This App

Registering process with PRIMUSS platform can sometimes be difficult for international students. To help them choosing the courses in english without hassle we have developed a web interface that can be accessed in every device (laptops, smartphones or tablets) without the need to install an app from platform's respective app-store.

Technologies

Our app is developed with Angular Framework. It is the most preferred framework for creating interactive components of a website. It was designed as a full-featured TypeScript framework to enhance simplicity and efficiency. Developers find Angular very effective especially in creating dynamic, single page apps, and supporting MVC (Model View Controller) programming structure. As the business environments become highly competitive, Angular has emerged as the super hero because of its intuitiveness and rich features.
